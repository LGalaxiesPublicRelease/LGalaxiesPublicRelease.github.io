Project: AGN LF evolution

Status

Ongoing

Collaborators

Daniele Spinoso, Silvia Bonoli, David Izquierdo-Villalba, Markos Polkas et al.

Description

The physical model which describes the mass-growth of SMBHs in L-Galaxies has been shown to produce a high number of active BHs at high redshift, potentially in line with recent JWST observations. This project aims at using L-GalaxiesBH to explor which are the driving factors of the quick growth and intense activity of massive BHs at z>3. We will focus in particular on the physical processes related to BH growth and evolution, such as the delay between galaxy mergers and BH growth, the spin evolution of BHs or their duty-cycle. The aim of the project is to understand the build-up of the faint-end of the AGN luminosity function across cosmic times.
