Seminar Series

Rationale & meeting link

The L-Galaxies Seminar Series is intended to be a collection of online seminars with a bi-monthly schedule, where speakers selected from within the L-Galaxies community will have the possibility to present their work to the whole community. The format of the seminars is as follows:
Invited talk (25 min) + Questions (5 min) + Discussion (30 min).

The Discussion session at the end is intended to focus on the relation between the topic of the talk and the L-Galaxies code, as well as (eventually) on the activity of the related L-Galaxies Working Group(s).

The date and time of each seminar will be announced to the general L-Galaxies mailing list.
