Project: Merger-induced DCBHs

Status

Ongoing

Collaborators

Daniele Spinoso, Lorenz Zwick, Pedro Capelo, David Izquierdo-Villaba, Silvia Bonoli et al.

Description

By leveraging the new L-GalaxiesBH version which includes the BH-seeding model presented in Spinoso et al. 2023 (project: BH seeding), we aim at studying the role of the physical model presented in Zwick et al. 2023 within cosmological contexts. This model envisions the formation of massive BH seeds induced by gas-rich galaxy mergers at high-redshift, hence being one of the viable scenarios for explaining how the first SMBHs came into existence at z>6. We explore the occurrence of this SMBH-seeding scenario on the Millennium simulation, whose merger trees have been extended (as presented in Bonoli et al. 2025) and initialized with the outputs obtained by L-GalaxiesBH run on the Millennium-II merger trees. This allows us to exploit at the same time the large cosmological volume of the Millennium and the good mass-resolution of the Millennium-II, an unprecedented feat for testing this SMBH formation model.
