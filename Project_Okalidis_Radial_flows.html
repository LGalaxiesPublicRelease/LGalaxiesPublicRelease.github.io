Project: Radial flows

Status

Ongoing

Collaborators

Periklis Okalidis, Guinevere Kauffmann, Rob Grand, Rob Yates

Description

We aim to update the recipe for the radial transfer of cold gas in the Henriques+19 version of L-Galaxies, as well as introduce the radial transfer of stars. We will base the implementations on an analysis of gas and stellar motions in the AURIGA simulation suite of disc galaxies.
