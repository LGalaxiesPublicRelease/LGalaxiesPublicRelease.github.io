Project: L-Galaxies & TNG

Status

Ongoing

Collaborators

Reza Ayromlou, Dylan Nelson, Rob Yates, Guinevere Kauffmann, Simon White

Description

We will compare galaxy properties from the Illustris TNG100 and TNG300 hydrodynamical simulations with those obtained from L-Galaxies run on the TNG dark-matter-only merger trees. This allows us to compare baryonic properties between models on a subhalo-by-subhalo basis. We will also make Local Background Environment (LBE) catalogues for the TNG100 and TNG300 simulations, to complement those made for Millennium-I & II in Ayromlou+19.
