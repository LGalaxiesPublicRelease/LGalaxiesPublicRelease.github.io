Project: Disc instabilities

Status

Ongoing

Collaborators

Dimitrios Irodotou, Peter Thomas, Bruno Henriques, Rob Yates

Description

Using the Henriques+19 version of L-Galaxies, we will extend the analytic global disc instability recipe described in Irodotou et al. (2018) to local scales (i.e. calculate the local Toomre Q parameter in each ring), in order to study the formation of bar-related pseudobulges. This prescription will include the formation of gas & stellar clumps, and their migration into the pseudobulge.
