Project: Dust & binary stars

Status

Ongoing

Collaborators

Rob Yates, David Hendriks, Aswin Vijayan, Rob Izzard, Payel Das, Peter Thomas

Description

Dust: We will implement an updated version of the Vijayan+19 dust production/destruction model into L-Galaxies 2020, thereby combining it with the radially-resolved rings formalism and improved GCE modelling. Recipes for the survival of dust in the hot CGM will also be incorporated.
Binary stars: The chemical yields and lifetimes for binary stellar populations from the binary_c stellar evolution code will be implemented into L-Galaxies 2020. This will allow us to (a) more accurately track chemical enrichment, and (b) consider a wider range of chemical elements and enrichment sources (novae, Wolf-Rayet stars, SNe-1bc, NS-NS mergers, etc).

Results form these two implementations will be compared to the latest observations of dust and chemistry at a range of redshifts.
