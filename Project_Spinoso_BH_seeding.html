Project: BH seeding

Status

On-going

Collaborators

Daniele Spinoso, Silvia Bonoli, et al.

Description

We implement prescriptions for the formation of super-massive Black Holes in the high-redshift universe in the Henriques+15 version of L-Galaxies. In particular, we model both the spatially-dependent IGM metal pollution from SNe feedback and UV background from short-lived massive stars in order to link the birth of BH seeds to the local physical conditions of the IGM at their formation site. Due to the high mass-resolution needed to resolve the collapse and early evolution of gas halos at z > 7, we run and test our model on the Millennium-2 merger trees.
