Workshop 2022

Information

We are very pleased to announce the final dates for the L-Galaxies workshop 2022:


Date: 16th & 17th November 2022 Location: Enterprise Hub @ University of Hertfordshire, UK


The workshop will include (a) presentations on the latest L-Galaxies code developments and results from various groups, (b) discussions about future plans for the model and possible collaborations, and (c) a "hackathon" session to get new users up-and-running with the latest public version of the code, L-Galaxies 2020.
