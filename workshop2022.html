Workshop 2022

Information

We are very pleased to announce the final dates for the L-Galaxies workshop 2022:


Date: 16th & 17th November 2022
Location: Enterprise Hub @ University of Hertfordshire, UK
Hosted by: The Centre for Astrophysics Research (CAR)


The workshop will include: (a) presentations on the latest L-Galaxies code developments and results from various groups,
(b) discussions about future plans for the model and possible collaborations, and
(c) a "hackathon" session to get new users up-and-running with the latest public version of the code, L-Galaxies 2020.


If you are interested in attending the workshop, either in-person or virtually, please contact the main organiser, Rob Yates, at r.m.yates -at- surrey.ac.uk
