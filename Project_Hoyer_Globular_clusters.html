Collaborators

Description

We implement the formation and evolution of various types of star clusters (young massive clusters, globular clusters, nuclear star clusters, ultra-compact dwarf galaxies) into the 2020 version of L-Galaxies. Special attention is set on the co-evolution of these star clusters with massive black holes.
