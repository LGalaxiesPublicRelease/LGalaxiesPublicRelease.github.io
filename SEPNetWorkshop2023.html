SEPNet Workshop 2023

Basic information

Date: 13:00 - 15:00, Wednesday 29th March 2023


We're looking forward to welcoming you to the workshop on "Running your own galaxy evolution simulation with L-Galaxies" at the SEPNet conference on "The Imitation Game: A growing affinity between simulations and observations of our Universe"! By the end of the workshop, you will have your own patch of Universe running on your laptop or computer cluster!
Installation & running instructions for the L-Galaxies code (for Linux, Mac OS, and Windows systems) can be found Installation & running instructions here.
In order for our time to be best used on the day, please complete up to Step 3 of these instructions before the day of the workshop. For the purposes of the workshop demo, just treefile 5 from Millennium-I is required (see step 2). If you have any issues, feel free to email me any time at: r.yates3 -at- herts.ac.uk
